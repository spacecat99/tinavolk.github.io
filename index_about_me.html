my path

"Hi, my name is Tina Volk, and I'm 24 years old. I have been living in Lucerne, Switzerland for a while now, and I absolutely love creating things. I have three cats and a beautiful vintage apartment in the city center. My sense of humor has been greatly influenced by classic cartoons on Cartoon Network, DreamWorks animated films, and animated sitcoms like Family Guy and BoJack Horseman. When it comes to movies, I'm particularly drawn to psychological thrillers with unexpected twists. That's the kind of element I like to incorporate into my designs - fun with a little twist.
I was born and raised in a small country in Central Europe called Slovenia. When I was 16, my family moved to Switzerland to pursue business opportunities. Shortly after that, I enrolled in high school in Lucerne and graduated in the summer of 2020. I was unsure about which career path to choose, but my passion for visual design and animation remained strong. While studying, I dedicated my free time to self-education and practice using various software tools like Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator, Clip Studio, and Adobe Animate.
Over time, I discovered UX Design, which immediately captivated my interest. I completed the 'Google UX Design Course' over the course of a year, learning a great deal about the workflow, thought process, and execution involved. I also took some programming courses to expand my programming skills. This allowed me to code in Java, JavaScript, Python, Pascal, as well as the essential HTML and CSS. By combining my design skills, love for animation, and theoretical and practical knowledge of Informatics, I found a way to express my creativity."
